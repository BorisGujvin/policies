Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Dittu ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application Dittu (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Permissions We Request

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Camera — used solely to enable core in-app functionality (e.g. capturing or scanning images). No photos or videos are stored on our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Microphone — used solely to enable audio recording or voice-related features within the App. Audio is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Bluetooth (HID) — used to connect the device as a Bluetooth Human Interface Device (e.g. keyboard or input controller). No Bluetooth data, device identifiers, or paired device information is collected, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

All requested permissions are used exclusively in real time, within the App, to provide the core functionality. No personal data, sensor data, or device data is saved to our servers or shared with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data on external servers. Any data processed by the App remains on your device and is not transmitted externally.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data-collection services.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
