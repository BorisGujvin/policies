Roadwhisper — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2025

Roadwhisper ("the app") is an AI-powered audio tour guide for Android. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Location Data

The app uses your device's GPS location to find nearby points of interest and generate audio stories about them. Location data is processed locally on your device and used only to query the OpenStreetMap Overpass API for nearby places.

We do not store, log, or transmit your precise GPS coordinates to our servers.

2. AI-Generated Content

When a nearby place is detected, a short description is generated by a third-party AI service (Groq / Anthropic Claude). The request sent to the AI includes:

  • The name and type of the place (e.g. "Stockholm Cathedral, church")
  • Approximate coordinates of the place (not your location)
  • The selected narration language

Your precise GPS location is not included in AI requests.

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (by Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. This helps us improve the app. Data collected includes:

  • App events (e.g. story narrated, place name, narration language)
  • Device model, OS version, app version
  • Crash reports and stack traces

No personally identifiable information is collected. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Push Notifications

The app uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to receive optional push notifications. No personal data is sent through this channel.

5. OpenStreetMap Data

Place data is fetched from the OpenStreetMap Overpass API. Queries include your approximate location to find nearby places. OpenStreetMap's privacy policy applies: osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy.

6. Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Selected narration language and voice preferences
  • Selected place categories
  • Diagnostic log files (optional, shareable by the user)

None of this data is uploaded to external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: contact@roadwhisper.app
